What do these intents mean?

  • 🟢 ENGAGED: Played a clip to completion, or listened to a substantial portion (5s+).
  • 🟡 EXPLORING: Rapidly cycled through variant files or modes in short succession.
  • 🔴 REJECTED: Interrupted the clip early (less than 2s) and switched types.
  • ⚪ UNKNOWN / ERROR: Default state or hardware/config error occurred during the session.
